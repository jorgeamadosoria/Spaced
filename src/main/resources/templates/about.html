This app is meant as a tracking site and showcase of personal projects.

Projects displayed range from POCs meant to explore a particular technology set, to passion projects or actual professional work. I have been an integral part of all of these projects, gaining insight and experience by developing, designing or otherwise working on each one of them, even though I'm not always the only or even main author.

Projects are most commonly code artifacts, but not necessarily so. They may or may not be available depending on the proprietary or free nature of the information and deliverables.

Projects can be:

ACTIVE

The project is currently being developed or actively maintained

DORMANT

The project is not finished yet, but there is no real activity going on. It may or may not be picked up in the future

PROSPECTIVE

The project exists only as an idea or high level design. Sometime in the future it may go into ACTIVE status, or disappear if the idea does not find a proper way to realization

FINISHED

The project has finished active development and is either abandoned or in maintenance-only mode
