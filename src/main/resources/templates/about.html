Spaced

This app is meant to be a study aid. It presents a selection of cards, starting by the newly added, followed by the ones more recently tried and failed, and finally the ones successfully tried.

This is very loosely based in the idea of spaced repetition, except with less sophisticated spacing. For an actual explanation of the spaced repetition technique, see this video:

  Spaced Repetition

This app is originally meant to help in memorizing French verbs declinations and vocabulary, but it can be used for any other set of knowledge that can be put to cards.
